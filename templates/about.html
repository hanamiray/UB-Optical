Our Story

Founded in 2013 by Dr. Romina U. Buenaobra, O.D.
U-B Optical began as a small vision care clinic with a big dream: to provide comprehensive, personalized eye care to our community. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted optical center known for our expertise, cutting-edge technology, and commitment to patient satisfaction.

"At U-B Optical, we believe vision care should be accessible, personalized, and delivered with compassion. Every patient who walks through our doors becomes part of our optical family."
